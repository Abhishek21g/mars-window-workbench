The next SpaceX question

SpaceX is not selling launches. They are selling a commit decision: can humanity afford to close this Mars window with hardware that exists today?

What we benchmark

  • Manifest mass, volume, crew, return propellant
  • Synodic departure window and transfer envelope
  • Tanker chain LEO fill timeline
  • TMI margin, EDL mass, surface power, consumables

What you get

  • mars-window plan|run|doctor|report
  • Receipt JSON in out/receipts/<run-id>/
  • 24 pytest on gate logic
  • Mock mode: no secrets, no proprietary sims

Honesty

Synthetic physics envelopes only. Not affiliated with SpaceX. Inspired by public multiplanetary mission statements and receipt discipline from fprime-preflight / Tinker Workbench.
